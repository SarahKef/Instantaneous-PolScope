Citation: 
This software was written to enable calibration of instantaneous fluorescence polscope and analysis of data acquired with it. The method and its applications are described in the following article:
Mehta, Shalin B.,et al. ‘Dissection of Molecular Assembly Dynamics by Tracking Orientation and Position of Single Molecules in Live Cells.’ Proceedings of the National Academy of Sciences 113, no. 42 (October 18, 2016): E6352–61. doi:10.1073/pnas.1607674113.
